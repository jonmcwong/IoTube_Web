Connecting Commuters and TfL Engineers

This underground monitoring system will make commuters journeys more comfortable and help TfL Engineers with underground maintenance. A common issue with the tube is the lack of consistent and accurate monitoring of the tunnels and stations, leading to poor ventilation, tube breakdowns and delays and in many cases, leading to the compromise of commuters health and well being.

This system will help TfL Engineers better detect particular tube stations that require immediate attention and can also indicate congested and endangered tube routes if a mains gas or water pipe leaks into the tunnels or stations.
